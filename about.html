About us

Gab lotto is an official lottery company in Nigeria, founded in 2007 and fully licensed by the National Lottery Regulatory Commission. Specialised in delivering a vast array of gaming solutions to its customers, Gab Lotto is passionate about availing Nigerians with the opportunity to turn their dreams into reality.

At Gab Lotto, we maintain our customer-focused operations by empowering our sales forces with the skills and knowledge to deploy gaming platforms and frequently train our principal agents to effectively use our platforms and a technical team on standby to resolve technical challenges, making it possible for stakers and agents to experience our games glitch-free and conveniently.

Your satisfaction is our goal!
